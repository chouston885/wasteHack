Where can I Recycle in Belfast?

This maps displays the Recycling Centres across Belfast, as well as their status.

Click the icons below to find out more about the statuses. We define the status by three parameteres...

  • 2 Centresthumb_upGREEN

    If a Recycle Centre is GREEN, this means it is open, not currently busy and it has good capacity.

  • 0 Centresthumbs_up_downAMBER

    If a Recycle Centre is RED, this means the centre is open, but may be busy or have limited capacity, or both.

  • 1 Centrethumb_downRED

    If a Recycle Centre is RED, this means the centre is either closed, have no capacity, or both.
